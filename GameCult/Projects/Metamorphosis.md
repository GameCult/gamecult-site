# Metamorphosis

Metamorphosis is the stream-integrated creature-collection idea in the GameCult orbit: accessible, social, and built around live audience participation instead of solo progression.

The older pitch imagined it as a Twitch extension game where viewers could capture and train adorable creatures, battle on stream, and keep coming back because the channel itself became the play space. That made it attractive both as a game concept and as a streamer tool: more retention, more audience interaction, and a cleaner reason to hang around between bigger on-stream moments.

It is still best described here as the studio-facing overview. If the project grows into a deeper public design surface later, this page should stay the simple answer to "what is it and why does it belong here?"
