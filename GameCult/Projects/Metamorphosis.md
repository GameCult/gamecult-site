# Metamorphosis

Metamorphosis is creature collection for people whose natural habitat is somebody else's chat window: accessible, audience-driven, and designed around participation instead of solo progression.

As a Twitch extension game, it lets viewers capture and train adorable creatures, battle on stream, and keep coming back because the channel itself becomes part of the play space. That makes it valuable on both sides of the screen: more audience interaction for the streamer, more reasons to stay for the viewers, and at least one retention mechanic that is not spiritually dead.

The pitch works because the loop is simple, social, and legible at a glance. It should be easy to understand in seconds, fun to invest in for weeks, and pretty enough that the stream feels more alive with it running.
