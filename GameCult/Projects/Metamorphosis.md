# Metamorphosis

Metamorphosis is the stream-integrated creature-collection project in the GameCult orbit: accessible, social, and built around live audience participation instead of solo progression.

As a Twitch extension game, Metamorphosis lets viewers capture and train adorable creatures, battle on stream, and keep coming back because the channel itself becomes the play space. That makes it attractive both as a game concept and as a streamer tool: more retention, more audience interaction, and a cleaner reason to hang around between bigger on-stream moments.

Because the mechanics are simple, social, and easy to understand at a glance, it also makes a strong case as a streamer-friendly product rather than only a standalone game. The idea is to give viewers a reason to stay, play, and support the channel while something playful and beautifully rendered unfolds on stream.
