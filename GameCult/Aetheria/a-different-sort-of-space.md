# A Different Sort of Space

<figure class="gamecult-media-card">
  <img src="../media/aetheria/aetheria-space.png" alt="A stylized Aetheria space scene." />
</figure>

The old site described Aetheria's visual direction with refreshing honesty: it was not trying to be hard science fiction.

Instead, Aetheria aimed for a stylized vision of space where gravity wells could become literal wells, scale could bend when it served the experience, and the void could feel inviting instead of sterile. The point was atmosphere and wonder, not realism for its own sake.
