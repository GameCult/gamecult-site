# End of the Line

<figure class="gamecult-media-card">
  <img src="../media/aetheria/catastrophe.jpg" alt="Aetheria concept art showing catastrophe-themed imagery." />
</figure>

Aetheria: Terminus is the first major step into the wider Aetheria universe: a distilled version of larger long-term plans delivered in a challenging rogue-lite ARPG structure.

The objective is simple and strong. Travel through a hostile procedurally generated galaxy, survive its unpredictable challenges, and make your way to the enigmatic Terminus. Megacorporations, hostile conditions, and the general indifference of space all stand between the player and freedom.
