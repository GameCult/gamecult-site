# Aetheria

*"Aetheria is bigger than a wiki, and its publishing surface should be bigger than one too."*

Aetheria is GameCult's flagship world and most ambitious project: a science-fantasy setting built to support fiction, worldbuilding, and multiple styles of play. The dedicated site at `aetheria.gamecult.org` is the deeper home for lore, essays, fiction, and longer-form publishing. This section keeps the concise studio overview close at hand.
